BMI Categories and What They Mean

  • Underweight: BMI < 18.5 — You may need to gain weight. A balanced diet and consultation with a doctor are recommended.
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5–24.9 — You’re within a healthy weight range. Maintain a healthy lifestyle!
  • Overweight: BMI 25–29.9 — You may be at risk of health issues. Consider diet and exercise adjustments.
  • Obesity: BMI ≥ 30 — Increased risk of health problems like diabetes and heart disease. Seek medical advice.
